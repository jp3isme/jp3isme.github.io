Bio

I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Georgia, passionate about our future and the applications of technology. I began programming over 6 years ago with small websites and then moved on to iOS application development. Since then, I've developed a specific interest in the applications of artificial intelligence (AI) and autonomous technologies. I believe these are going to change the world in the coming years to a greater extent than most people realize. For this reason, I'm enormously interested in their development and safety. I started college at Kennesaw State University in 2015, then transferred to the University of Georgia in the fall of 2017 to focus on AI. I'm on track to graduate with my B.S. in Computer Science in 2019, then I plan to further my education by getting my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence.
